We’re pleased to announce the successful deployment of the new **Keylo News Center**.

This release introduces a centralized communications hub designed to improve transparency, streamline updates, and ensure all stakeholders remain aligned with platform developments.

## What This Means

The News Center enables us to:

- Publish structured product updates
- Share feature releases and improvements
- Communicate operational changes in real time
- Provide clear documentation of enhancements

## Platform Enhancements

As part of this deployment, the system now supports:

- Rich Markdown formatting (bold, italic, headings, code blocks)
- Banner images per announcement
- Tag-based categorization
- Static optimization for fast global delivery

All future updates will be distributed through this channel to ensure consistency and accessibility.

## Our Commitment

We remain committed to delivering a reliable, scalable, and transparent platform experience. The launch of the News Center represents another step forward in strengthening communication across the Keylo ecosystem.

Further updates will be shared here as new features and improvements are deployed.

Thank you for your continued support.
